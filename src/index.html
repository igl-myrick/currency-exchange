Currency Exchange

USD Conversion

Convert United States Dollars into another currency by writing a number in USD to convert, then the name of another currency (using 3-letter codes).

Available currencies for conversion from USD:

  • AUD (Australian Dollar)
  • BRL (Brazilian Real)
  • CAD (Canadian Dollar)
  • EUR (Euros)
  • GBP (British Pounds)
  • HKD (Hong Kong Dollars)
  • JPY (Japanese Yen)
  • NOK (Norwegian Krone)
  • SEK (Swedish Krona)
  • SGD (Singapore Dollar)
